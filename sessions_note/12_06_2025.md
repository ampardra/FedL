# Session Note 1

## Guiding Questions for Reading a Research Paper

When reviewing a paper, consider the following questions:

1. **What research question does this paper aim to address?**
2. **Which problems does the paper successfully solve or partially address?**
3. **What aspects or metrics does the proposed method improve?**
4. **What trade-offs or limitations does the method introduce?**
5. **What opportunities exist for further improvement or future work?**
